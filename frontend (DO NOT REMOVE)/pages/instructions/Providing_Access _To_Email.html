Providing Access to URMC Email

  1. Open Outlook application
  2. Right Click on Email you would like to give access for
  3. Click Folder Permissions
  4. Click Add
  5. Type the person name in
  6. Double click their name
  7. Click Ok
  8. Person Name will apear in box
  9. Click on their name
  10. Select the check box with permission they should have
  11. Click Apply
  12. The person will now have those permissions
