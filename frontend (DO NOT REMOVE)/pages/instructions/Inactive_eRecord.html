Check Status of eRecord account

  1. Open eRecord
  2. Search for the user by username
  3. Status column should show inactive ( If they are showing active they might not know their password )
  4. Check sumtotal to make sure they have completed all of their trainings
  5. ( Tranings not complete ) -> They will need to complete them before getting access to eRecord

    ( Traings Complete ) -> Ticket will go to eRecord_Security/SER_SN to activate their account
